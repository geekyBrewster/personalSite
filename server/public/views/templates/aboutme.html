About Me

For the six and a half years prior to beginning my web development bootcamp at Prime Digital Academy, I was an adjunct instructor at IPR - College of Media Arts. It was a great six and a half years working with not only wonderfully creative students but also truly amazing faculty and staff. That position also managed to utilize the bulk of my random knowledge and experience, ranging from film studies, video production, and Adobe Creative Suite to theatrical lighting design, corporate audio-video skills, and computer programming.

When I'm not writing code, learning about new technologies, or being a nerd in general, I have a few hobbies and sports I do. Currently, I'm a lapsed homebrewer, but I do enjoy checking out new taprooms when I can, usually by bike. I knit various hats, blankets, and nerdy items for friends and family. To keep myself active, I play Ultimate frisbee when it's nice outside and broomball when the snow falls. Occasionally, I'm an amateur photographer.

StrengthsFinder: Intellection, Harmony, Input, Relator, Responsibility

Fun fact: Grew up on a farm raising llamas, sheep, and pigs

Favorite Fictional Character: Spock. He's calm in a crisis and analyzes a situation before jumping in.
